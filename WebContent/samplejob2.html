The job

We’ve been building and selling our products for the past five years with a small dedicated team and a cutting edge Java/Spring stack. We’re now looking for a new developer who takes pride in his work to join our team and contribute to our future product developments.

  • Develop a web-based product using the SCRUM agile methodology.
  • Program on a bleeding-edge Java application based on the Spring Framework and many other open-source technologies.
  • Code both on the front (JavaScript, JSP, Controllers) and backend (Services, DAO).

The company

MicroSigns is specialized in electronic shelf labels for the cell phone stores of major carriers. We replace paper fact tags with interactive mini-kiosks, one for each cell phone in the stores. We also provide a system to centrally manage content and product placement. We design, develop and manufacture everything: from hardware video players, to wireless access points, to SaaS web applications in the cloud.

Why join us?

  • We react fast, communicate a lot, and there are no politics!
  • Developers are expected to be thinkers and designers (not just doers). You’re expected to be a professional, and we treat you as such.
  • You’ll get to work with and learn the latest and greatest technologies.
  • We’re a small and fun team, yet we’re big enough to offer job stability, good processes, insurance and reasonable work hours.
  • We continuously build on our products so we keep our code clean and pay our technical debts.
  • We’ve got brand new, bright and spacious offices; but we’re very flexible on when and where you work. We provide a laptop, a huge monitor and a headset.

A day at work

  • Daily scrum meeting. Keep it short.
  • Work on stories, spikes, bugs. Test your work!
  • Participate in tech chats to discuss/criticize technical issues and improvements. Convince others through argument and technical merit. Show some attitude…
  • Work in our new offices or work from wherever you want. Keep in constant touch with our chat rooms, Skype, IM, etc.

How we will evaluate your work

  • Be a professional! Give your opinion on technology and methodology; admit it when there’s a better solution. Hiding problems bears the penalty of death.
  • Frequent, precise and to-the-point communications with fellows.
  • High quality and consistency: coding conventions, naming, methodology, etc. Keep the code clean.
  • Product oriented. This is not a short service mandate: you’re in for the long run.

What we expect

  • Excellent programming skills with Java 5 and/or 6.
  • Strong skills with Spring Framework and Spring MVC
  • Strong skills with XML, HTML and CSS
  • Good database skills (SQL, Hibernate/ORM)
  • Experience with a JavaScript framework such as YUI, jQuery or Dojo
  • 5 years of industry experience with web-based Java development.
  • Speak and write both French and English
  • Professionalism, passion and attitude!
